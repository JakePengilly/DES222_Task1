VR Experiences
Virtual reality offers users a unique opportunity to simulate a vast amount if differing experiences and virtual locations. VR provides interactive virtual environments where individuals around the globe can share in the excitement of meeting new people, exploring new locations and engaging in the activities as if they were physically together.
Virtual reality equipment comes in a variety of different price ranges, equipment specs and form factors, allowing users of all needs to find the device that most suits their desires. Most VR headsets do not require the device to be connected to a computer to perform optimally, this improves the immersion of a users experience as it allows them to move freely throughout their play space.
VR chat is by far the most popular virtual reality game, it is a community driven social game, it offers users the ability to interact with players from around the world in social zone called “Hubs”.
Rec Room is a virtual reality game focused on exploring the opportunities that VR creates. Rec Room is a social platform that allows users to play various games together and interact in real time with their shared virtual environment.
